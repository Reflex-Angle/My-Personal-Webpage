About Me

Ok let's get into this. Hi! Welcome to my personal webpage. I am Sriram and this is just a fun little project of mine. I find my life to be ordinary but don't we all? Maybe my life won't seem so ordinary to people such as you reading this. Hopefully this webpage should give you an insight into my (not-so-ordinary?) life.

I am an engineering student studying in VIT Chennai and I am pursuing Computer Science. I was born in Chennai but soon after, we moved to Dubai where we stayed for around 4 years. Came back to Chennai and studied in PSBB Millenium for the second grade. I had a fun time in PSBB till 7th grade after which I moved to the inner city. DAV was my new second home. As a happy alumnus of DAV Gopalapuram, I have had a prolific academic career and boast quite a few feats. Fast forward half a decade and here I am, studying software engineering. Opportunities are plentfiul now that I have joined the prestigious VIT university and now my dreams are my only limit.

I dabble in several sports in my free time but I consider myself to excel in football and badminton. Well, I guess certificates of accomplishment speak for themselves. I also play tennis, table tennis, chess and swim now and then. I love to read. Give me 20 solid story books and I'll bing through them in a month! Okay an exaggeration perhaps but you get the point.

So yeah, that's me. My webpage should fill you in on the rest.
